============================================================
Project: Human Pose Estimation & Skeletal Tracking
============================================================

By tracking 33 skeletal landmarks in real-time, this project provides 
the foundational technology for analyzing human movement, posture, 
and gestures. Below are the key real-world applications.

---

### 1. AI Fitness & Sports Coaching
* Exercise Form Correction: Analyzing a user's squat or push-up 
    technique and providing real-time feedback on their posture.
* Sports Performance: Tracking a player’s body angles during a 
    golf swing or a football kick to optimize performance.
* Repetition Counting: Automatically counting workout reps by 
    detecting when joints reach specific angles.



---

### 2. Healthcare & Physical Therapy
* Range of Motion (ROM) Testing: Helping physiotherapists measure 
    exactly how far a patient can bend their knee or lift their arm.
* Gait Analysis: Monitoring a patient's walking pattern to identify 
    irregularities or assist in recovery after an injury.
* Remote Monitoring: Allowing doctors to track a patient’s progress 
    at home through standard webcam-based assessments.

---

### 3. Gesture-Based Control (HCI)
* Touchless Interfaces: Controlling computers or smart devices 
    using body movements (e.g., swiping in the air to change slides).
* Interactive Displays: Creating museum or retail displays that 
    react when a person stands in a certain pose or waves.



---

### 4. Gaming & Animation
* Motion Capture (MoCap): A low-cost alternative to expensive 
    MoCap suits, allowing indie developers to map real human 
    motion onto digital characters.
* Augmented Reality (AR): Attaching digital clothes or effects to 
    the body in real-time for virtual try-on experiences.

---

### 5. Workplace Safety
* Ergonomic Assessment: Analyzing how employees sit or stand at 
    their desks to prevent long-term back and neck injuries.
* Heavy Lifting Alerts: Detecting if a warehouse worker is using 
    an unsafe posture while lifting heavy boxes to prevent 
    workplace accidents.



---

### Summary
This project transforms a standard webcam into a powerful motion 
sensor, enabling software to "understand" the human body's position 
and movement in 3D space.
